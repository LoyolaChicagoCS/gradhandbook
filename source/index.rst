#########################
Graduate Student Handbook
#########################

Welcome to the Graduate Handbook informational site for the Computer Science Department at Loyola University Chicago.

This site is aimed at helping current Graduate school applicants and those who are interested in the Computer Science Department's Graduate program offerings at Loyola University Chicago.

If you have questions about the information here, please contact the Graduate Program Director at gpd@cs.luc.edu or at (773) 508-8150.

.. note::

    See the main Computer Science Department web site for general program information and how to apply for admission to our undergraduate and graduate programs, including our professional certificate programs.

This site is also available in |pdf-version| and |epub-version| formats.
